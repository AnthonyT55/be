Weekly Maintenance

My most hands-on plan — I come out every week for chemical balancing, skimming, brushing, and vacuuming. Your pool stays perfect all season, no exceptions.

Bi-Weekly Maintenance

Every two weeks I handle a full top-to-bottom service — water chemistry, cleaning, and a complete check of your equipment. Consistent results on your schedule.

Monthly Maintenance

A thorough monthly service visit — full clean, chemical correction, and equipment inspection. Great for lower-use pools that still need professional upkeep.

Tile Cleaning

Calcium buildup, waterline staining, and grime don't stand a chance. I scrub your pool tile clean — it makes the whole pool look brand new.

Water Chemistry

Every plan includes precise pH, alkalinity, and sanitizer testing with full chemical correction. Balanced water every single visit — no guesswork, no shortcuts.

Client Education

I take the time to walk you through your system — how it works, what to watch for, and how to keep it running between my visits. Know your pool.

Pool Cover Box Cleaning

The cover box housing collects debris, algae, and buildup that can damage your cover and mechanism over time. I fully clean out the box, keeping your system running smooth and protected.
